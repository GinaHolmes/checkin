Privacy notice

When you complete this check-in, the following information is collected: your name, student ID, and your responses to the questions on this form.

This information is collected for the purpose of providing you with pastoral and academic support during your programme. It will be used only by your Programme Director to identify where support may be needed and to follow up with you appropriately.

Your responses are stored securely and will be retained for the duration of your programme, after which they will be deleted. Your data will not be shared with any third party, used for any purpose other than your individual support, or disclosed to others within the University without your knowledge except where there is a safeguarding concern that requires it.

You have the right to request access to the information held about you, to ask for it to be corrected, or to ask for it to be deleted. To exercise any of these rights, please contact your Programme Director directly.
